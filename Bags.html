About Us

Bags with Mulan is your one-stop shop for stylish and high-quality bags in Kenya. We offer a wide range of bags to suit every occasion, from casual everyday use to elegant evening events. Our mission is to provide our customers with fashionable and durable bags at affordable prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • expand_more What are your delivery options?

    We offer free delivery within Nairobi. For other areas in Kenya, we use courier services with delivery times ranging from 2-5 business days.

  • expand_more Do you offer returns or exchanges?

    Yes, we offer returns and exchanges within 14 days of purchase, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging.

  • expand_more How can I track my order?

    Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS that you can use to track your package.
